* North
    * [Capernaum National Park](https://en.parks.org.il/reserve-park/kfar-nahum-capernaum-national-park/) (North of Lake Kinneret, ruins + church above the ruins.

    * Mitzpe Ramon

## Public Transportation in Israel

Probably the best is to use one of the applications to see the lines, the schedules and to pay.
I personally use the [HopOn](https://hopon.co.il/) application to pay for public transportation. You pay as you go and it automatically applies the daily or monthly maximum payment.

There is also [Rav-Kav](https://ravkavonline.co.il/en/).

There are several bus companies serving different areas of the country and there is [train service](https://rail.co.il/). Some of these sites are blocked outside of Israel.

* [Egged](https://www.egged.co.il/eng/) bus service.
* [Kavim](https://www.kavim-t.co.il/) bus service.
